iframe fix? Essential Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)

Personal Protective Equipment represents a critical component of forklift safety protocols. Each piece of PPE serves specific protective functions and must be properly maintained and worn at all times during forklift operations. Understanding not just what to wear, but why each item is essential will help ensure consistent compliance with safety requirements.

PPE Item
Purpose/Requirements
Safety Shoes/Steel-toed Boots
Essential for protecting feet from the forklift's heavy weight and falling materials. Provides necessary grip on various surfaces and protects against electrical hazards.
Hard Hat
Crucial in warehouse environments where materials are stored at height. Choose Class E hard hats for additional electrical protection when working with electric forklifts.
High-visibility Vest
Critical for maintaining visibility in low-light conditions and busy warehouses. Choose Class 2 or Class 3 vests based on workplace risk assessment.
Safety Glasses
Protect against not just debris, but also battery acid during charging operations and hydraulic fluid during maintenance.
Work Gloves
Select task-appropriate gloves - mechanical gloves for maintenance, chemical-resistant gloves for battery handling, and cut-resistant gloves for material handling.
